About This App

Little Lights Bible Bedtime is a free iOS app designed to help children develop healthy bedtime routines through gentle Bible stories and relaxing audio. We are committed to protecting children's privacy and comply fully with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Our Privacy Promise

  • No Personal Data Collected — We don't ask for names, emails, birthdates, or any personal information
  • No Tracking — We don't track your child's activity or behavior
  • No Analytics — We don't collect analytics or usage data
  • No Ads — The app is completely ad-free
  • No Third-Party Services — We don't use external analytics, advertising, or tracking services

Information Collection

The Little Lights Bible Bedtime app collects zero personal information from users or their devices. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Birth dates or age information
  • Device identifiers or IP addresses
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavior tracking
  • Device photos, contacts, or other personal data
  • Information about which stories are read or how long they're used

Data Storage

The app stores data only on your device using Apple's local storage system (UserDefaults) to provide these features:

  • Reading Progress — Remembering which Bible stories you've listened to
  • Favorites — Saving your child's favorite stories for quick access
  • App Settings — Storing preferences like sleep timer duration and text size
  • Audio Playback State — Remembering where your child left off in a story

All of this data stays on your device and is never sent anywhere. Your child's preferences remain private and personal.

No Account or Sign-Up Required

Little Lights Bible Bedtime requires no account creation, no sign-up, and no login credentials. The app works completely offline and doesn't need an internet connection for its core functionality. Download the app, open it, and start enjoying Bible stories immediately.

Audio Content

All audio files (Bible stories and background sounds) are bundled with the app during installation. They are stored locally on your device and do not require downloading from the internet. This ensures fast loading, reliability, and complete privacy.

Children's Privacy Protection (COPPA)

Little Lights Bible Bedtime is designed for children and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We understand that children's privacy is extremely important to parents. Here's how we protect it:

  • No Personal Data: We never collect any personal information from or about children
  • No Behavioral Tracking: We do not monitor, track, or record how children use the app
  • No Marketing: We do not use any information for marketing or advertising purposes
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Because we collect no data, there is nothing to share with third parties
  • Parental Supervision: While the app is child-friendly and doesn't require monitoring, we recommend parental guidance as part of healthy device use practices

Third-Party Services

Little Lights Bible Bedtime does not use any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Amplitude, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • No advertising networks (Google AdMob, Facebook Ads, etc.)
  • No tracking libraries (Facebook SDK, etc.)
  • No social media integrations
  • No crash reporting services
  • No cloud storage or syncing services

The app is completely independent and self-contained. Data never leaves your device.

Security

Your device's security settings protect any data stored locally in the app. We recommend:

  • Keeping your iOS device updated with the latest security patches
  • Using a device passcode or Face ID/Touch ID to protect your device
  • Regularly reviewing app permissions on your device settings

Your Control

You have complete control over the app and your data:

  • All stored data (favorites, progress, settings) is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • You can clear the app's data at any time through iOS Settings > Little Lights Bible Bedtime > Offload App or Delete App
  • You can disable app permissions for microphone, contacts, photos, etc. in iOS Settings at any time
  • No data backup or syncing occurs without your explicit action

## FULL DESCRIPTION
(4000 characters maximum)

Good night, sweet child. Welcome to FireFly: Bible Bedtime Stories—where ancient Bible stories become gentle companions for peaceful slumber.

Bedtime can be a challenge. FireFly is a completely free app that turns Bible stories into a soothing nightly ritual, helping children ages 3–12 wind down, feel secure, and drift off to sleep wrapped in God's love.

**50 Beautiful Stories, Fully Narrated**
Every story is specially adapted for young listeners—calming, age-appropriate, and filled with timeless wisdom—with its own illustration, a Talk About It question, a bedtime prayer, and a memory verse. Stories are organized into 7 themes: Trust, Courage, Peace, Love, Hope, Prayer, and Kindness. Warm AI narration feels like a caring adult reading at bedtime, and read-along highlighting helps children follow the words.

**Record Your Own Voice**
With Parent Voice, a grown-up can record any story in their own voice—and that's what your child hears at bedtime, even on nights you're away. Recordings stay on your device, always.

**Made for Sleepy Eyes and Lock Screens**
Narration keeps playing with the screen locked, complete with lock-screen controls. Lights Out mode darkens the whole screen while the story plays—with an optional Kid Lock so little fingers can't wake it. A sleep timer fades stories out, Sleepy Speed slows narration to an extra-gentle pace, and 6 ambient sounds carry your child the rest of the way into sleep. Turn on Wind-Down and the app dims itself into Bedtime Mode when bedtime arrives, with Tonight's Story ready and waiting.

**A Complete Bedtime Routine**
Story → Prayer → Breathing → Blessings. Guided breathing with Lumi the firefly teaches children a calming skill they can use anytime.

**Play & Learn: 7 Bible Games**
A whole Games tab of gentle, story-based games—Story Quiz, Who Am I?, Lumi's True or False, Guess the Story, Treasure Match, Story Scramble, and Verse Builder—with hundreds of questions covering all 50 stories. Every ending is encouraging; there are no trick questions.

**Rewards That Build Healthy Habits**
Sleep Stars, 10 firefly levels, reading streaks, weekly challenges, 30 badges, and 50 story treasures to browse in the Collection Book. In Lumi's Night Sky, your child decorates their very own sky with 100 stickers and the treasures they've earned—across 7 dreamy backdrops—then saves their creation to Photos.

**7-Day Journeys & Tonight's Queue**
Themed week-long story journeys give bedtime a gentle plan, and Tonight's Queue chains up to three stories back-to-back into ambient sound.

**Made for the Whole Family**
Up to four child profiles, each with their own streaks, favorites, and collections—synced across your devices privately through your own iCloud. You can even ask Siri to "Play tonight's story."

**100% Free. Zero Ads. No Surprises.**
No ads, no in-app purchases, no paywalls. We believe quality bedtime content should be accessible to every family.

**Your Child's Privacy is Sacred**
COPPA-compliant by design. No accounts, no tracking, no data collection—progress lives on your device and in your family's own iCloud. Parent Voice recordings never leave your device.

FireFly is more than an app—it's a gift you give your child each night. Download today. It's free. It's safe. It's love, one story at a time.

## APP REVIEW NOTES (App Store Connect → App Review Information)

FireFly: Bible Bedtime Stories is a children's Bible bedtime story app. It is 100% free with no ads and no in-app purchases. It is COPPA-compliant and collects no user data: no accounts, no analytics, no third-party SDKs, no developer server.

Permissions and entitlements, and exactly why each exists:
- **Microphone** — used only by the optional, parent-initiated Parent Voice feature, where a grown-up records story narration. Recordings are stored on-device only and are never uploaded anywhere.
- **Photo Library (add-only)** — used only when the child taps the camera button in Lumi's Night Sky to save a picture of their sticker scene to Photos. The app never reads the photo library.
- **iCloud Key-Value Storage** — syncs reading progress, favorites, and collections across the user's own devices. No data ever reaches the developer.
- **Background audio** — bedtime narration continues with the screen locked (lock-screen Now Playing controls).

## MARKETING COPY TONE & VOICE

**Overall Approach:**
- Warm, reassuring, and parent-focused
- Emphasize safety, quality, and faith
- Build trust through transparency (free, no ads, COPPA-compliant, on-device)
- Balance emotional appeal (bedtime ritual, security, love) with practical benefits (lock-screen playback, sleep timer, Wind-Down, routine structure)
- Avoid hard-sell tactics; invite families to experience the app as a gift

**Key Messages to Reinforce:**
1. ✅ 100% free, forever—no hidden costs
2. ✅ Privacy-first—no accounts, no tracking, recordings stay on-device
3. ✅ Age-appropriate—designed for children 3–12, gentle everywhere (even the games never sting)
4. ✅ Bedtime-engineered—lock screen, Lights Out, Kid Lock, Wind-Down, sleep timer
5. ✅ Personal—Parent Voice, per-child profiles, their own Night Sky
6. ✅ Faith-building—stories, prayers, memory verses, Talk About It questions
7. ✅ Holistic bedtime—story + prayer + breathing + blessings

**Copy rules (owner directives — apply to ALL marketing surfaces):**
- Never "hiding/hid God's Word in your heart" → say "learning by heart" / "missing word"
- Never the word "Affirmations" → say "Blessings"
- Never position Lumi as false/wrong/negative (the game is "Lumi's True or False", never "True or Lumi?")
